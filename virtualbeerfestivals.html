Virtual Beer Festivals

Ever fancied having your own beer festival, but don’t know where to start? Don’t worry, we will take care of it all for you.

All you need to do is decide how many barrels of beer you want and which beers from our massive selection you would like to order. We can help you decide on quantities and types of beer so you don’t get stuck with too much, or worse still, run out early.

The minimum order is just 2 barrels of craft beer. We will deliver it to your venue, along with everything you need to stack it, cool it and pour it. We even set it all up for you.

All you have to do is drink it. Your guests will be able to pour their own beer and you can just sit back and watch people have a great time and claim all the glory for yourself.

The day after your party, we will come and pick everything up and take it all away.

This free service is available anywhere in a 10 mile radius of the Craft Beer Shop. For venues further away, just ask us for a quote.
